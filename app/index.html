How to Play

Move orbs to form a line of five of the same color. The path the orb takes must be clear.

Wilds match any orb.
Exploders cause adjacent orbs to disappear when matched with their color.
Destroyers cause all orbs of that color to disappear.
Fixed Orbs can not be moved, but you can make a line around them.

Point System

Removing orbs awards points per line, increasing with difficulty. 5 on easy, 10 on medium, and 20 on hard.

Bonus points are awarded for removing multiple lines at once (50 per extra line), or removing extra orbs in a line (5 per extra orb).

Multipliers apply when removing consecutive lines.
