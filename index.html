    About Hex-Lines

    Hex Lines is a casual game where the goal is to create lines of 5 buttons of the same color. Special buttons act as wildcards, allow you to explode neighboring buttons, or destroy all buttons on the board that match that color.

    History

    Hex Lines was developed at Google's Sydney office, Australia under the BOLD Internship Program. Completed early 2013.

    Authors and Contributors
